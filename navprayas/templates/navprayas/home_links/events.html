MTSE

MTSE which stands for Manpur Talent Search Examination, is the flagship event of Navprayas. This is the one of the regular events of Navprayas conducted each year to ignite the flame of competition from junior classes itself. We have objective type question paper and GD/Extempore/speech and interview in the first and second round respectively for classes 5 to 10.

Eligibilty -
1. Students should be a citizen of India.
2. Participants should be enrolled in any Educational Institutions(schools/coaching).
3. Students currently studing in class 5 to 10 are allowed to enroll for this examination.

Exam pattern - Objective type Questions
Stages - 2(Mains and Internview)
Mode of examination -Offline(Pen & Paper)
Language - Hindi and English
Syllabus - Maths, Science, Social Science, English, General Reasoning from respective classes.
Registeration fee - 25.00 INR

Puzzle Race

This event is organised for the students of the class 7 to 10. Main objective of this event is to develop the ability of puzzle solving skills among students.

Eligibilty -

2. Participants should be enrolled in any Educational Institutions(schools/coaching).
3. Students currently studing in class 5 to 10 are allowed to enroll for this examination.

Exam pattern - Objective type Questions
Mode of examination - Offline(Pen & paper)
Language - Hindi & English
Stages - 1
Team members - 3(max.) and 2(min.)
Syllabus - Maths & General Reasoning
Registeration fee - 10.00 INR

Free hand sketching

It has been introduced by Navprayas group since 2008. It is the most interesting and creative event among students to involve them in creative ideas through painting, sketching and took their attention on social, environmental and political problems apart from their regular studies.

Eligibilty -
1. Candidate should be a citizen of India.

Rules -
1. Use of Pen, colors, electronic gadgets, cheats etc. are not allowed inside hall of examination.
2. Sketching should be related to any themes provided by Navprayas Team.

Career Counselling

Main motive is to give proper guidance, elaborate rules of counselling and help them get the best college and desired department according to their achieved rank.
