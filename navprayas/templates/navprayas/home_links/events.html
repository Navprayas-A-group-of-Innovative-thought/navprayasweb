MTSE

MTSE which stands for Manpur Talent Search Examination, is the flagship event of Navprayas. This is the one of the regular events of Navprayas conducted each year to ignite the flame of competition from junior classes itself. We have objective type question paper and GD/Extempore/speech and interview in the first and second round respectively for classes 5 to 10.

Eligibilty -
1. Students should be a citizen of India.
2. Participants should be enrolled in any Educational Institutions(schools/coaching).
3. Students currently studing in class 5 to 10 are allowed to enroll for this examination.

Exam pattern - Objective type Questions
Stages - 2(Mains and Internview)
Mode of examination - Online
Language - Hindi and English
Syllabus - Maths, Science, Social Science, English, General Reasoning from respective classes.
Registeration fee - 25.00 INR per candidate
