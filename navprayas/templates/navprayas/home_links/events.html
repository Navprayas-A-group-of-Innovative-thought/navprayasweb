MTSE 2021

About
MTSE which stands for Manpur Talent Search Examination, is the flagship event of Navprayas. This is one of the regular events of Navprayas conducted each year to ignite the flame of competition from junior classes itself. We have objective type question paper and GD/Extempore/speech and interview in the first and second round respectively for classes 5 to 10.

Total number of rounds- 2(Mains and Interview)
Mode of examination- Online
Language- Hindi and English
Subjects: Science, Maths, Reasoning, S.St, G.K(current affairs), English

Fee- Free of Cost


Eligibilty
1. Participant should be a citizen of India.
2. Participants should be enrolled in any Educational Institutions(schools/coaching).
3. Students currently studing in class 5 to 10 are allowed to enroll for this examination.

NavRachna

Create with what you have

About
This event is mainly being conducted for those people who want to create some mini projects based on their creative and innovative skills. One has to make models with the materials available in their home (some of them have been mentioned below). So, what is a model? Most of you would have heard it for the very first time. Model is a 3D presentation for something that you want to propose or something which you want to make in real life. It should be smaller and less complex than real-life projects. For example, if we want to make a car-model, then we just have to show its structure rather than showing the whole concept. We have to depict everything but it doesn't mean that we have to make the real things.

Important Dates
Registration starts: 28th May, 2021
Registration Deadline: 15th June, 2021
Submission Period: 4th June, 2021 to 18th June, 2021
Stage 1 Result- 24th June, 2021
Questionnaire Session: 27th June, 2021
Final Result Release: 3rd July, 2021

Eligibility
No class and age limit.
Group of max. 3 members(from same family/house to avoid the spreads of COVID-19)
Parents can also take part.
